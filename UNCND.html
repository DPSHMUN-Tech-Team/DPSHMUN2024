UNCND

UNCND is one of the functional commissions of the Economic and Social Council of the UN, and is the central narcotic drug policy-making body within the UN System. The commission supervises the international efforts to control the use and movement of narcotics and psychotropic substances while assisting the ECOSOC in overseeing the implementation of international drug control treaties.

Agenda

Assessment of the world drug problem with a particular focus on the supply reduction of emerging drug threats.
