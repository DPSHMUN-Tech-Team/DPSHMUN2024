ECOFIN

ECOFIN, is the second committee of the United Nations General Assembly. This committee addresses issues related to economic growth, sustainable development, globalization and eradication of poverty. It discusses policies and solutions for various matters in the financial and economic sphere.

Agenda

Promotion of international cooperation to combat illicit financial flows and strengthen good practices on assets return to foster sustainable development.
